Welcome to the Tutorial

User Scenarios

In this game, you'll encounter different user scenarios, ranging from planned crimes to friendly jokes. Take your time to go through them carefully.

Review Details and Information

Take your time to examine materials like user emails or transactions. Pay attention to any alerts or important information. Missing crucial details could impact your decision and lower your score.

Make Your Judgment!

After reviewing the information, it's up to you to decide whether the individual is innocent or requires further consideration. Your decision will affect your score, so choose wisely.

Scoring

After making your choice, you'll earn points based on correctness and speed. Correct answers and quick responses earn more points. You can also earn bonus points for consecutive correct answers, or lose points for consecutive incorrect ones.
