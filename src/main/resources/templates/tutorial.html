How to Play

Scenarios

Throughout the game, you will encounter a variety of scenarios, ranging from planned crimes to harmless pranks. Take your time to assess each scenario carefully, reviewing all available information before making a judgment. You will have 5 minutes to consider each scenario, so make sure to use your time wisely!

Review Details and Information

Examine all relevant materials, including user emails and transaction records. Pay close attention to any alerts or important details, as overlooking crucial information could negatively impact your decision and result in a lower score.

Make Your Judgment!

After thoroughly reviewing the information, it's up to you to decide whether the individual is innocent or requires further investigation. Your decision will directly impact your score, so choose wisely!

Scoring

Upon making your decision, you will earn points based on accuracy and speed. Correct answers and quick responses will earn you more points. Additionally, you can earn bonus points for consecutive correct answers or lose points for consecutive incorrect ones.
