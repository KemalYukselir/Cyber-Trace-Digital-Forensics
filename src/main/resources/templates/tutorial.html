Guide to Digital Forensics

1: Introduction To Digital Investigations

In the world of digital forensics, every piece of data tells a story. Step into the shoes of a digital detective as you navigate through various scenarios, uncovering clues, and solving mysteries.

2: Analysing With The Given Resources

Examine the given information carefully, dissecting emails, transaction, records, and unraveling hidden messages. Your keen eye for detail is your greatest asset.

3: Making Informed Decisions

You have two options to pick from, escalate and not suspicious. You wiill be scored based on the impact you make

4: Race Agaisnt Time And Difficulty

You will have 5 minutes to make use of the score multiplier. Every minute, the multiplier goes down by one. Your score will also be affected by the difficulty of the question (ranging from difficulty 1 to 3). Every decision you make get multiplied by the multipler and difficulty

5: Leaderboard

At the end of the game, if you reach a high score, this will be displayed on the leaderboard page. Make sure to beat your friends!
