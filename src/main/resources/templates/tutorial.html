Tutorial

Goal and Scoring

You will have to go through a bunch of user scenarios where you will have to make a descision on this particular scenario. You may be wrong or right and you will be scored based on your performacne which will then go on the leaderboard. Get investigating!

Review Details and Informations Showcased

There are many materials and resources to go through in the game, e.g the users emails or transactions. Make sure to carefully read through these informations and pick up on any alerting information. You may miss any information that would of been crucial.

Make Judgment!

After thoroughly reviewing the information and user details, it is your choice to determine whether the individual is innocent or warrants further consideration. You will then be scored on this decision so be careful.
